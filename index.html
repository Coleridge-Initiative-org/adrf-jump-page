    2. Export Process Reminder
    Export requests may take up to 5 business days for primary review. If required, it may require a secondary review by the sponsoring agency.

    Delays can occur if modifications are required. Export requests can be rejected. Coleridge Initiative is committed to aid you in making any adjustments as needed.
    How to check your Export Review Status: If you submitted an export request, you can check the review status of your submission by opening the ADRF Export module in the ADRF. Ensuring integrity and compliance is our priority. If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to reach out via email to support@coleridgeinitiative.org.
